Why I Began my Coding Journey...

After a 15 year career in the service and hospitality industry, I found myself at a crossroads. I had lost my job due to the covid-19 pandemic, as so many others did, and found myself feeling lost and helpless. I knew it was time for a big change. I've always loved problem solving, and was looking to find something that would finally help me to exercise my brain again after many years of exhausting physical labor, ungrateful customers, disgruntled co-workers, and menial tasks.

Epicodus has provided me with exactly the opportunity I was looking for. For the first time in a long time, I'm facing daily challenges that are actually engaging. I'm truly enjoying problem solving with my fellow students in the pair programming model, and working with a new partner every day gives me access to a wealth of knowledge and experience that my classmates possess. I'm beyond grateful to have this opportunity, and am looking forward to a bright future in the tech industry!
